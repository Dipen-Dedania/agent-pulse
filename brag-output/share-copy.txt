You step into a meeting trusting your agent to ship the feature — and come back to find it stalled on a permission prompt the whole time. Or worse: it ran `rm -rf /` on Auto mode. Agent Pulse pings you the second an agent needs you — a desktop bubble you read like a traffic light (🟢 working, 🟠 idle, 🔴 stopped) — and its guardrails block destructive commands before they run. 100% local, no telemetry.
